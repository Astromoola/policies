Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my session?
    Go to the Sessions tab, select your active session, and tap "Reset".
  • Why doesn't audio play in the background?
    Enable Background App Refresh under Settings → Stellar Manifestation → Background App Refresh.
  • How can I change my focus type?
    Navigate to Home > Profiles, tap your profile, and select a new focus under "Manifestation Focus."
  • Where can I find my progress streak?
    Your day counter appears on the bottom of the Player screen—tap the loop icon to view session stats.
  • Where is my data stored?
    All session and profile data is securely stored in your iCloud account or on-device if iCloud is disabled.
