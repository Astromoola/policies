Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Profile Data

  • Birth Details: Name, date, time, and location of birth. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Optional Label: Custom labels for distinguishing multiple profiles.

All birth details are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted or stored on external servers.

1.2 Usage & Analytics Data

  • Anonymous analytics events (screens, taps, feature usage) via Firebase Analytics.
  • No personally identifiable information is associated with these events.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate personalized charts & playlists based on your birth details.
  • Schedule local reminders at your chosen times.
  • Improve the app using anonymous usage data.
  • Store birth details only on-device; no external backups.
  • Develop features with anonymized insights (with permission).

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Birth details & session progress stored in a local Hive database.
  • No transmission or remote backup of personal birth data.
  • Analytics sent to Firebase in anonymized form.

4. Data Sharing

  • We never sell, rent, or share personal birth details.
  • We share only anonymized analytics with Google (Firebase Analytics).
  • Crash reports sent to Firebase Crashlytics for diagnostics.
  • Internal processing of anonymized data for feature support only.

5. Your Choices

  • Delete Profiles: Remove any profile permanently from your device.
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Toggle “Share usage data” off in Settings.
  • Manage Notifications: Adjust notifications in your system settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our app evolves. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes. Please review periodically.
